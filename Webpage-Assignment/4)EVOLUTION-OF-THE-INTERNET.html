EVOLUTION OF THE INTERNET

The Internet is constantly evolving, and this evolution is perceived, as changes to the software and networked application or also the introduction of a foreign concept, to improve the communication, entertainment, etc. The Internet seems to be growing more pervasive and critical infrastructure, that also seems to be underpinning society and commerce in and around the world. It is important for technologists and policymakers alike, to understand the way the Internet grows.

“Evolution occurs as a response to a stimulus. In the Internet context, these come in the shape of challenges for which improvements are needed, as well as bright ideas for which implementations are sought.” (Internet Society Panel on Internet Evolution @ IETF 81, 2011)
